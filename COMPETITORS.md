# RiskLattice Competitors & Market Position

## Direct Competitors

### 1. **Riskalyze**
- **What they do**: Risk scoring and portfolio risk analysis for financial advisors
- **Price**: Enterprise/Professional ($$$)
- **Strengths**: Professional-grade risk analytics, widely used by advisors
- **Weaknesses**: Expensive, not for individual investors, complex UI
- **Our advantage**: Free, simpler, AI-powered news analysis

### 2. **TradingView**
- **What they do**: Charts, technical analysis, some risk metrics
- **Price**: Free tier + Premium ($$)
- **Strengths**: Great charts, large community, many indicators
- **Weaknesses**: Not focused on risk scoring, premium features cost money
- **Our advantage**: Focused specifically on risk, AI news analysis, clearer risk explanations

### 3. **Finviz**
- **What they do**: Stock screener with risk/volatility metrics
- **Price**: Free (basic) + Elite ($$)
- **Strengths**: Powerful screening, lots of data
- **Weaknesses**: Cluttered UI, not intuitive, no AI analysis
- **Our advantage**: Clean UI, AI-powered, explainable risk scores

### 4. **Yahoo Finance**
- **What they do**: Basic financial data, news, some volatility metrics
- **Price**: Free
- **Strengths**: Comprehensive, well-known, free
- **Weaknesses**: Not focused on risk, cluttered interface, no risk scoring
- **Our advantage**: Dedicated risk scoring, AI analysis, cleaner interface

### 5. **Portfolio Visualizer**
- **What they do**: Portfolio risk analysis and backtesting
- **Price**: Free (basic) + Premium ($$)
- **Strengths**: Advanced portfolio analytics
- **Weaknesses**: Complex, focused on portfolios not individual stocks
- **Our advantage**: Simpler, stock-focused, real-time analysis

## What Makes RiskLattice Different

✅ **AI-Powered**: Combines market data with news sentiment analysis  
✅ **Explainable**: Shows WHY a stock is risky, not just the score  
✅ **User-Friendly**: Clean, modern interface focused on risk  
✅ **Free**: No premium tiers or hidden costs  
✅ **Real-Time**: Live data updates, not historical only  
✅ **Focused**: Built specifically for risk analysis, not general finance  

## Market Position

**Target Users:**
- Individual investors who want to understand stock risk
- Traders who need quick risk assessments
- Anyone building a watchlist and wants risk intelligence

**Positioning:**
"The simplest way to understand stock risk - AI-powered, explainable, and free"
